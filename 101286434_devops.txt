System administrators (SysAdmins) and those completing development and operations (DevOps) tasks are essential in the information and technology industry to ensure a company's network, hardware and software operate properly. As technology continues to advance and companies rely more on computer systems to function efficiently, businesses have begun hiring both of these professionals at higher rates. If you're interested in becoming an IT professional, it may be helpful to compare the duties of a SysAdmin and DevOps personnel to determine which role is right for you. so in short words this course (COMP 2156) specially designed for that.
